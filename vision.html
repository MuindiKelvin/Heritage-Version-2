Our Vision

To be a comprehensive, care,protection,empowerment and transformation children/youth centre.

Our Mission

Transforming lives of orphans and vulnerable children through care, providing quality education and teaching them the word of GOD

Mission Statement

It's not the will of the father who is in heaven that one of these little ones should perish.
Matthew 18:14.

ORGANIZATIONAL STRUCTURE

The centre has good governance structure in place. Since inception,the founders have embraced good management practice.The policy formulation and advisory role is carried out by a committed board of trustees drawn from different proffessions.The daily operations and the smooth running of both the childrens' home and the school are supervised by a team of management led by the Executive Director, Finance Director,Project Manager and the School Principal

OBJECTIVES(i)

Our main goal is to do holistic ministry for the children which includes;
A.Physically:by giving them food,clothing and shelter.
B.Emotionally:by giving them love,a hearing ear for the cry,hope for the future and a shoulder to cry on in the time of distress.
C.Spiritually:by teaching them how to pray, read the word of God and need on dependence on his provision.

OBJECTIVES(ii)

D. Academically:by giving them quality education through admitting them to schools colleges and universities.
E.Morally:by giving them guidelines for acceptable behaviour and building a character in them.
F.Empowerment:by assisting them through skill development and admitting them into technical training to become more self reliant.
G.Awareness:by teaching them on their rights and responsibilities.
