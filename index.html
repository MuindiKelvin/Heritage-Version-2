Services

As we work to attain our vision, we also provide the following services to our children

Guidance and Counselling

The Bible is our principle when it comes to guidance and cancelling. Our dedicated team has a wide range of experience and work day to day for the best interest of the child.

Provide a home for OVC

There is no place like home. Here at Heritage of hope we offer shelter and food to the OVC for these reasons they have a place to call home.

Educate the OVC

We believe it is the right of every child to access education and for these reasons we give the best education to our ability. We use the 8-4-4 syllabus.

Care for OVC

James 1:27 Religion that God our father accepts as pure and faultless is this: to look after orphans and widows in their distress and to keep oneself from being polluted by the world.

Drama Festivals

We also embrace Arts and our children have been currently participating in different aspects of arts.

Farming

The organisation also practices farming as a source of investment as well as providing food to the children.

Communicate To Us

We always appreciate the incredible support as well as your feedback and therefore we would like to give you an opportunity to communicate with us on anytime through our platforms.
